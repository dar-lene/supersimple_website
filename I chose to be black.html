The "I Chose to be Black Collection" is a powerful painting project revolving around the impactful movement of Black Lives Matter. It goes beyond exploring the movement's broader impact and delves into the artist's personal journey and inspiration towards self-realization.
Through a series of meticulously crafted paintings, the collection aims to depict the struggles, resilience, and achievements of Black individuals and communities. Drawing inspiration from Black history, the artworks highlight the importance of understanding and learning from the past, empowering viewers to embrace their own identities and work towards a more inclusive future.
By intricately weaving narratives from the past with contemporary experiences, the "I Chose to be Black Collection" prompts viewers to confront social and racial injustices. It serves as a catalyst for introspection, encouraging individuals to embrace their unique identities and contribute to a more equitable society.
Ultimately, the collection stands as a visual chronicle of the beauty and resilience of Black culture, conveying a resounding message of unity and compassion as we collectively strive for a more inclusive and just world.
